Run the memory export prompt

This pulls everything your current AI knows about you and gets it ready to bring into Claude. ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, doesn't matter which one you use.

1
Open whatever AI you use the most.
2
Switch to the smartest model you have, or turn on thinking / reasoning mode. You'll get a fuller, more accurate export. In ChatGPT, switch from Auto to Thinking:
AutoThinking

(5)
Required · Step 5 of 6

Save it into a document

When it's done, copy the entire output and paste it into a Word doc or Google doc. Save it somewhere easy to find, and bring it Thursday.

We'll edit this together on the call, then load it into Claude. This becomes Claude's memory of you, so it knows who you are from day one.
(6)
Required · Step 6 of 6

Grab a writing sample

Find one or a few things you've written in your own voice. Emails, posts, blogs, anything. It has to be something you actually wrote, not AI, not your assistant.

Drop it in a doc you can grab fast on Thursday. We'll use it to teach Claude to sound like you, not like a robot.

Nothing written down? Open your sent email folder and find an email you wrote that has a bit of length to it. That works great.
